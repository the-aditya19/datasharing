Resume Template Cloner

Paste your template resume (A) and source resume (B). The tool keeps A's section order, fills matching headers from B, and removes non-matching sections with no blank gaps.

How header matching works
  • Header names are normalized (case-insensitive, punctuation ignored).
  • Aliases are supported (e.g., "Work Experience" == "Experience").
  • If a header from A does not exist in B, it is removed entirely.
